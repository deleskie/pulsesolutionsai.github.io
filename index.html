About Pulse Solutions AI

Pulse Solutions AI is the umbrella company behind James DeLeskie’s consulting practice and innovative automation software. We help businesses—from lean startups to hyperscalers—secure their digital infrastructure and harness the power of AI.

Our mission is to merge decades of internet routing and network‑security expertise with forward‑thinking AI solutions. We believe in transparent, candid advice that doesn’t sugar‑coat the realities of modern cyber threats. Every engagement is tailored to your unique needs, ensuring scalable and sustainable results.

Meet the Founder

James DeLeskie is a seasoned network and security architect with more than 25 years in the trenches. In the 1990s he helped build some of the earliest ISPs in Canada and Ireland. He later led global network‑security efforts at Tata Communications, where he oversaw the largest DDoS detection and mitigation platform of its time.

As founder of Heimdall Networks and later Mimir Networks, James pioneered affordable anomaly‑detection services. Today he works at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, crafting scalable, cloud‑native defenses against DDoS attacks and advising clients on network design. His passion for heavy‑metal music fuels a relentless pursuit of innovation and authenticity.
