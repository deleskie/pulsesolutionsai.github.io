About James DeLeskie

Founder & Chief Architect

James has spent over 25 years pushing the boundaries of networking and security. Now at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, James designs cloud‑scale defences and continues his mission of making the internet safer and more reliable. Outside of work, he’s a passionate heavy‑metal guitarist—channeling that energy into every project.
