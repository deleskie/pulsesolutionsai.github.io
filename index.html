About Pulse Solutions AI

Pulse Solutions AI is a consulting and technology firm dedicated to helping organizations of all sizes build secure, intelligent networks and automation systems. We integrate decades of internet routing and cloud-network expertise with cutting‑edge AI to deliver robust solutions for startups, enterprises and hyperscalers.

Our approach is forward‑thinking and candid: we identify weaknesses, design scalable architectures and implement defenses without sugar‑coating the reality of modern cyber‑threats.

Our Services

  • Internet Routing & Architecture

    We design and optimize IP networks, from ISP backbones to enterprise WANs. Our team provides guidance on BGP routing policies, peering strategies, network visibility and building resilient global infrastructures.

  • Private LLM Training & Inference

    Harness the power of large‑language models on your own code and data. We set up private model‑training environments using your repositories, fine‑tuning models for specific domains, and deploy inference pipelines that respect privacy and scale efficiently.

  • DDoS & Security Consulting

    Protect your business from distributed‑denial‑of‑service attacks and other threats. Our experts analyse traffic patterns, deploy anomaly‑detection systems and craft multi‑layered mitigation strategies based on decades of hands‑on experience defending hyperscale networks.

  • Automation & Marketing Solutions

    We deliver AI‑driven workflows that automate operational tasks and enhance marketing reach. From intelligent monitoring to campaign optimization, our platform accelerates growth without revealing proprietary product names.
